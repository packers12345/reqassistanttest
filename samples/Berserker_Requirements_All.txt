[1.4.1]  (Single Maintenance Port)
  All components of the MQ-99 connected to the 1553 bus shall be accessible via a single maintenance port to facilitate forensics and maintenance.

[1.4.1.1]  (Software Loading)
  All loadable firmware for components attached to the 1553 bus shall be loaded via the maintenance system connected to the single maintenance port that will be accessible both when the air vehicle is containerized for storage, and assembled for flight.

[1.4.1.2]  (Software Loading Switch)
  Software shall only be permitted to be loaded when the system is put into a maintenance mode via a guarded physical switch that will be automatically switched to flight mode when the maintenance panel is closed.  This permission will be provided via a discrete signal instead of a message over the bus that could be spoofed and this switch will be accessible both when the air vehicle is containerized for storage, and assembled for flight.

[1.4.2]  (Maintenance Loading System Capabilities)
  A maintenance loader system shall be provided to download and upload data from the MQ-99 Air Vehicle.

[1.4.2.1]  (Maintenance System Complete Load)
  The maintenance loader shall be able to load all loadable software and firmware on the MQ-99 Air Vehicle through a single user-initiated process that will take no more than 60 minutes to complete.

[1.4.2.2]  (Maintenance System Partial Load)
  The maintenance loader system shall be able to load any single piece of software or firmware on the MQ-99 Air Vehicle.

[1.4.2.3]  (Maintenance System Download)
  The maintenance system shall be able to download files or data from any components of the MQ-99 Air Vehicle attached to the 1553 bus.

[1.4.2.4]  (Maintenance System Data Dumping Capabilities)
  The maintenance system shall be able to read and copy the physical memory space of each MQ-99 component attached to the 1553 data bus.

[1.4.3]  (Software Update Timeline)
  The prime contractor shall be able to produce emergency updates to software on the MQ-99 in response to identified vulnerabilities or cyber attacks within 72 hours.
